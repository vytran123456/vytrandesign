About Me
These are the projects and art I made in the past.

These days, being good at graphic design can be very useful. It's used everywhere, from the patterns on your McDonald's cups, bags, and wrappers to your favorite app on your phone, so it doesn't matter how good you are at it. There is a lot to learn in this field, but it can also be used to help other people. There's design for social justice, for small businesses and individuals, for nonprofits and groups that do good in the world, and so on.

Graphic design can be thought of as a way to solve problems. It's nice to be able to think of a way to solve a problem and come up with one! When I work as a graphic designer, I can take someone's problems and turn them into something helpful and beautiful.
